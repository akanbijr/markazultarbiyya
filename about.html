What is Markazul Tarbiyya?

Markazul Tarbiyya is a non-profit Islamic school committed to providing quality education with a strong emphasis on moral values. Our mission is to offer accessible education to all, regardless of financial limitations, by keeping our school fees highly subsidized at just â‚¦500. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to receive an enriching education rooted in the teachings of the Qur'an and the Sunnah. Our journey began in 2009 when a generous brother provided us with our current location. Inspired by the vision of establishing a center of excellence for Islamic education and propagation, we laid the foundation of the mosque, which quickly became a gathering place for congregational prayers and spiritual development. In this nurturing environment, the seeds of our informal Islamiyyah were sown, initially serving a small number of children from the local neighborhood. By the grace of Allah, in 2010, our mosque was completed, marking a significant milestone in our journey. Recognizing the need for a formal Islamiyyah, we launched our school that same year with two dedicated teachers and 15 students. However, our commitment to providing quality education led to a rapid increase in enrollment, and we soon faced the challenge of accommodating students with varying needs and capabilities. To address these challenges, we took a proactive step and constructed a block of two classrooms along with a shop in 2011. This expansion allowed us to create a conducive learning environment that caters to the diverse requirements of our students. We strive to provide an inclusive education that nurtures their academic, spiritual, and social development, shaping them into well-rounded individuals.

At Markazul Tarbiyya, we are driven by our passion to raise morally sound individuals who embody the teachings of the Qur'an and the Sunnah. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to instilling in our students a love for learning, a strong moral compass, and a deep understanding of Islamic principles. We encourage active participation, foster critical thinking skills, and cultivate a sense of social responsibility within our students. Our school is not just an educational institution; it is a community that values collaboration, respect, and compassion. We believe in the active involvement of parents, teachers, and students, as we work together to create a nurturing and supportive environment. Join us at Markazul Tarbiyya as we continue our journey to provide affordable, quality Islamic education. Together, let us raise a generation of individuals who are academically accomplished, morally upright, and committed to serving their communities in accordance with the teachings of Islam.
